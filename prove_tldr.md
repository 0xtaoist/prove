# PROVE - TLDR for team

Coins that stick. Creators that stay. Communities that hold.

---

## Why

98.6% of Pump.fun tokens rug. Average lifespan is 3-7 days. Bots eat 40-60% of launch volume. Creators dump and ghost. Nothing sticks. Not because the ideas are bad but because the launchpad is built for extraction.

We think the problem isn't the people. It's the launchpad.

---

## What we believe

Coins deserve a fair start, not one decided by bots in the first 10 seconds.
Creators should earn by building, not by dumping.
Fewer tokens, more attention.
Holding should be the smart play.
Signal over noise.

---

## Six mechanics

**1. Fair start** - 5-min batch auction. Everyone gets the same price. No bots. Minimum 50 wallets + 10 SOL or the launch fails and refunds.

**2. Skin in the game** - 2 SOL deployer stake. Returned if 100 holders in 72hrs. If not, forfeited to tokens that did make it. Bad launches fund good ones.

**3. Creators earn by building** - 0.8% of every trade goes to the creator. $50K daily volume = $400/day. Walk away and it stops. Build and it keeps paying.

**4. The 1% fee** - Same as PumpSwap. Split: 0.8% creator, 0.2% protocol. We take the smallest slice. Most creator-friendly split in the market.

**5. Signal over noise** - Feed only shows surviving tokens. Ticker registry prevents copycats. You see what's working.

**6. Prove score** - Wallet reputation across launches. Hold longer, participate more = recognized. Decays if you stop. Capped so newcomers catch up.
